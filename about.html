GET YOUR DREAM ROLE WITH US

AuditionMe is Sri Lanka’s first dedicated audition platform built to connect aspiring actors, models, voice artists, and performers with casting directors, producers, and agents across the country.Whether you’re looking for your first break or searching for the perfect talent, AuditionMe offers a space where dreams meet opportunities. From teledramas and films to advertisements and photoshoots, we bring together everyone involved in the performing arts industry.We’re here to make the casting process transparent, accessible, and efficient — for everyone, from Colombo to Jaffna.
